Cvpaper Challenge Conference and MIRU Mentorship Program (CCCMMP)

The “Cvpaper Challenge Conference and MIRU Mentorship Program (CCCMMP)" is a Japanese symposium on image recognition and understanding, as well as language, audio, robotics, and machine learning. Whether you belong to a university, a private company, or simply have a keen interest in cutting-edge research within this domain, CCCMMP provides an ideal platform for networking, collaboration, and socializing with fellow researchers and industry professionals.
